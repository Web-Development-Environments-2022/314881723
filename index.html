1950-1954

Rachel and Menashe Yacob's parents immigrated from Iraq to Israel. *Menashe lived in the Har Tov camp
*Rachel lived in Hatikva Quarter
They knew each other and got married in 1954.After the wedding they settled down in Hatikva Quartervs

1960

Yacob was born.
childhood memory: "a happy childhood, most of the day we play in the street and go a lot to the market that was identified with unique Iraqi food. At the end of the day-when was a game of Bnei Yehuda We went up to the neighbor's roof to watch the game."

1973-1977

During the Yom Kippur War, Yaakov was 13 years old. He remembers a mess in the streets and no one understood what was happening - "it was a huge chaos".
Yacob attended in Jewish educational institution until he was 14 years old. By this time he was also an apprentice in the Working Youth.
He issued his matriculation certificate at Shevach School, after much effort and a lot of investment in studies

1978

Yacob enlisted in Israel Defense Forces in the Nahal Brigade when he was 18 years old. He took part in a unique program in the core of Nahal, a combination with life on the kibbutz Hagoshrim. He was trained on a Tarkoker and worked in the kibbutz's agricultural fields in the 4th year of service
During the Galilee Peace War, Yacob was a medic in the Medical Battalion - treating the wounded at a field hospital established in Lebanon

1983-1988

Yacov lived 2 years in U.S.A-he traveled and work with friends from the military service. When he came back to Israel he learned electronics and teaching studies in Ort singalovski . After he finish the specialization in teaching studies, he started to teach at Makif Yehud High School-and he met the love of his life Shoshana Who later became his wife for life

2022

These days Yaakov is a grandfather to 2 amazing grandchildren, a father to 3 children and he had experience of over 30 years in the field of teaching, which included, among other things, the management of a stratum of about 500 students and the management of the journey to Poland.
